Research Profile

Working at the intersection of game theory, AI economics, market design, and operations management. Research focuses on strategic information aggregation, collective decision-making, and allocation mechanisms under uncertainty. Bridging theoretical economics with practical applications in healthcare markets, crowdfunding, and hiring platforms.
