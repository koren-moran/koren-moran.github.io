Research Profile

My research interests lie at the intersection of economic theory, operations management, and computer science. My work focuses on market design, strategic information aggregation, and mechanism design under uncertainty, particularly in scenarios where a tension exists between individual incentives and optimal collective outcomes. I apply theoretical tools from game theory, social learning, and machine learning to analyze and propose solutions for real-world allocation challenges in domains like healthcare markets, hiring platforms, and crowdfunding.
